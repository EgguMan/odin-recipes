Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Marscapone
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Ladyfingers
  • Coffee Liquor

Steps

  1. Mix the Heavy Whipping Cream, Vanilla Extract, Marscapone, and Granulated Sugar
  2. Using a mixer, beat the Heavy Whipping Cream until stiff peaks start to form. It is important to not over-beat.
  3. Put your cream to the side, and pour the coffe liqour in a not-too-deep bowl.
  4. Prepare a well-greased baking dish
  5. Dip, don't submerge, your Ladyfingers into the liqour one side at a time. First the front, then the back. Do this for one layer of the Ladyfingers at the bottom of your baking dish
  6. Cover the first layer of Ladyfingers in cream
  7. Repeat with multiple layers until no ladyfingers remain
  8. Finally, cover the final layer with cream, then dust that cream with Cocoa powder
  9. Cover with Plastic Wrap and let cool in the fridge overnight.
